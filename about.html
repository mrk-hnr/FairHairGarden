Learn About Us

To fill the gap in the market of hairdressing at affordable prices, a group of young inspiring fashionistas had founded Fair Hair Garden.
As there is an increasing amount of unmet desire, Fair Hair Garden had opened its doors to provide high quality, contemporary hair care in Taytay Rizal.

Our mission is to provide world-class service where the fusion of fashion and professionalism work to create the ultimate salon experience. To upgrade the level of hairdressing in the country by being pioneers in educational attainment and transfering the knowledge gained to aspiring beauty stylists.
